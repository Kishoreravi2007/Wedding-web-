# WeddingWeb Company & Website Overview

## Company Snapshot
WeddingWeb delivers a modern wedding-technology platform powering more than 10,000 events across 50+ countries while maintaining 99.9% uptime and managing over five million photos. The brand blends artistry with innovation, focusing on three pillars: preserving memories, enabling seamless collaboration, and providing enterprise-grade trust and privacy. The mission is to empower couples and professionals with intuitive digital experiences; the vision is to become the world’s most trusted wedding platform. Core values include Passion for Memories, Innovation First, Customer Obsession, and Trust & Security.

## Platform Experience
- Six marketing pages—Landing, About, Services, Pricing, Portfolio, Contact—live in `frontend/src/pages/company/`, each designed with responsive layouts, gradient branding, Framer Motion animation, and shadcn/ui components.
- The landing page converts visitors through a bold hero, live business stats, six-feature spotlight, customer testimonials, and multiple “Get Started Free” calls-to-action.
- The about page tells the WeddingWeb story from 2023–2025 milestones, clarifies mission and vision, and introduces leadership alongside success metrics.

## Products & Services
- AI-Powered Face Detection with privacy-first processing and instant guest photo discovery.
- Smart Photo Gallery offering flexible storage, auto-categorization, and high-resolution downloads.
- Live Streaming with HD/4K delivery over a global CDN for unlimited viewers.
- Photographer Portal enabling bulk uploads, automatic face processing, and client management workflows.
- Digital Wishes for personalized guest messages, multi-language support, and voice submissions.
- Event Management tools spanning interactive timelines, countdowns, RSVP management, and integrated reminders.
- Additional capabilities cover analytics dashboards, security controls, social sharing, CRM hooks, payments, and chat integrations.

## Pricing & Monetization
- Starter Plan: $99/month (or $999/year) supporting up to 100 guests, 5 GB storage, and essential features with email support.
- Professional Plan: $199/month (or $1,999/year) for up to 500 guests with live streaming, 50 GB storage, and priority support—flagged as the most popular tier.
- Enterprise Plan: $499/month (or $4,999/year) with unlimited guests and storage, 4K streaming, custom branding, and 24/7 dedicated management.
- Revenue expands through annual prepay discounts, add-ons (extended storage, professional videography, custom domain, advanced analytics), enterprise white-label packages, and professional services such as onboarding and training.

## Social Proof & Trust Signals
- Portfolio case studies highlight measurable outcomes, including a 94% average guest engagement rate, 2-hour average processing time, and 4.9/5 satisfaction score.
- Testimonials from couples and planners reinforce credibility, while visuals showcase real events.
- Security commitments emphasize bank-level encryption, GDPR compliance, secure transfers, and granular privacy controls.

## Target Markets & Marketing Strategy
- Primary audience: tech-forward engaged couples aged 25–35 in urban India planning 200–500 guest celebrations.
- Secondary audiences: event planners, destination specialists, venues, and photographers.
- SEO-ready site structure, fast load times, and semantic content support organic acquisition.
- Conversion levers include prominent CTAs, free trials, demo scheduling, lead forms, and social proof.
- Recommended social presence spans Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, and Pinterest for storytelling, professional relationships, and community building.

## Summary
WeddingWeb offers an end-to-end wedding experience platform that fuses AI-driven photo intelligence, global live streaming, and collaborative event management with a polished, conversion-focused marketing site. The company’s differentiators—no-app access, professional portals, and multi-language support—position it to scale subscription revenue while delighting couples and industry partners worldwide.
