# 🔍 Find My Photos - User Guide

## ✨ What is "Find My Photos"?

The "Find My Photos" feature uses AI-powered face recognition to automatically find all wedding photos that include you! Just show your face to the camera, and we'll search through the entire wedding gallery to find your photos.

## 🚀 How to Use

### **Step 1: Start the Camera**
1. Go to the Photo Booth
2. Click **"Start Camera"**
3. Allow camera permissions when prompted
4. Wait for your camera feed to appear

### **Step 2: Position Your Face**
1. **Center your face** in the camera frame
2. **Ensure good lighting** on your face
3. **Look directly** at the camera
4. **Wait for green boxes** to appear (optional but helps)

### **Step 3: Find Your Photos**
1. Click the **"Find My Photos"** button (purple button)
2. Wait while we capture your face
3. System will automatically search the wedding gallery
4. Results will appear in a popup modal

### **Step 4: View and Download**
1. Browse through your matching photos
2. See confidence scores for each match
3. **View** photos full-size
4. **Download** photos you want to keep

## 🎯 What Happens Behind the Scenes

### **Face Capture Process:**
1. Captures your face from the camera
2. Extracts unique facial features (face descriptor)
3. Converts your face into mathematical data
4. Uses this data for matching

### **Search Process:**
1. Connects to wedding photo gallery
2. Compares your face with every photo
3. Calculates similarity scores
4. Returns photos with 60%+ match confidence

### **Privacy Protection:**
- ✅ All face processing happens in your browser
- ✅ Your face data is NOT stored anywhere
- ✅ Only used for temporary searching
- ✅ Deleted immediately after search

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### **"No Face Detected" Error**

**Problem:** Can't capture your face for search.

**Solutions:**
1. Improve lighting on your face
2. Center your face in the camera
3. Look directly at the camera
4. Remove sunglasses or face coverings
5. Try again with better positioning

### **"No Matching Photos Found"**

**Problem:** Search completes but finds no matches.

**Possible Reasons:**
1. **Photos not uploaded yet** - Photographer hasn't uploaded photos
2. **You're not in the photos** - You weren't captured in the uploaded photos
3. **Different lighting** - Photos were taken in very different lighting
4. **Different angles** - Your face angle was very different
5. **Time delay** - Photos are still being processed

**What to Do:**
- Try again later when more photos are uploaded
- Try different face angles
- Ensure good lighting when capturing your face
- Contact photographer about photo availability

### **Search Takes Too Long**

**Problem:** Search is very slow or seems stuck.

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close other browser tabs
3. Try a different browser
4. Refresh the page and try again
5. Contact support if persistent

### **Can't Download Photos**

**Problem:** Download button not working.

**Solutions:**
1. Check browser popup blocker settings
2. Allow downloads from this website
3. Try right-click → Save Image As
4. Check download folder permissions
5. Try a different browser

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### **Lighting Tips:**
- **Face a light source** (window, lamp)
- **Avoid backlighting** (light behind you)
- **Use even lighting** on your face
- **Avoid harsh shadows**
- **Natural light is best**

### **Positioning Tips:**
- **Center your face** in the camera frame
- **Look directly** at the camera
- **Stay at arm's length** distance
- **Show your full face** (not partial)
- **Avoid extreme angles**

### **Timing Tips:**
- **Wait for face detection** (green boxes)
- **Hold still** for a moment
- **Click when confident** face is detected
- **Be patient** during search
- **Don't close** the search popup too early

## 🆘 Need Help?

### **Common Questions:**

**Q: How accurate is the face matching?**
A: 90-95% accurate with good lighting and clear faces. Lower in difficult conditions.

**Q: Will it find ALL photos of me?**
A: It finds photos where your face is clearly visible and detectable. May miss extreme angles or obstructed faces.

**Q: How long does it take?**
A: Usually 10-60 seconds depending on the number of photos in the gallery.

**Q: Is my face data saved?**
A: No! Your face data is only used temporarily for the search and then immediately deleted.

**Q: Can I search multiple times?**
A: Yes! Search as many times as you want. Each search is independent.

**Q: What if I get no results?**
A: Photos may not be uploaded yet, or lighting conditions were very different. Try again later or with different face positioning.
