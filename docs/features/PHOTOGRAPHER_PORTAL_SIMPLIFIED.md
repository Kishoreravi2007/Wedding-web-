# ✅ Photographer Portal Simplified

## What Was Removed

**"Gallery View" tab** - Removed from photographer portal

## New Tab Structure

The photographer portal now has **3 tabs** (simplified):

### 1. Upload Photos
- Upload new photos to Sister A or Sister B
- Drag & drop or browse
- Progress tracking
- Success notifications

### 2. Recent Uploads
- Shows last 5 uploaded photos
- Photo thumbnails
- **Actions:** View, Download, Delete
- File info (name, size, time)

### 3. Manage Photos ← Main Photo Management
- **Two galleries:** Parvathy's Gallery & Sreedevi's Gallery
- View all photos in grid
- Search by filename
- **Actions per photo:**
  - View (opens in new tab)
  - Download (saves to computer)
  - Delete (removes permanently)
- Full-screen photo viewer
- Photo counts displayed

## Why This is Better

### Before (4 tabs):
- Upload Photos
- Recent Uploads
- Manage Photos
- Gallery View ← Removed (redundant)

### After (3 tabs):
- Upload Photos
- Recent Uploads

**Gallery View was redundant** - Manage Photos does everything better!

## Manage Photos Tab Features

This single tab now provides complete photo management:

### Gallery Selection
- **Parvathy's Gallery** (Sister A) - 3 photos
- **Sreedevi's Gallery** (Sister B) - 17 photos
- Photo counts shown
- Easy switching

### Photo Grid
- Responsive layout (2-5 columns)
- Photo thumbnails
- Hover effects
- Click to view full-size

### Per-Photo Actions
- **View** - Opens in new browser tab
- **Download** - Saves to computer
- **Delete** - Removes with confirmation

### Search
- Filter photos by filename
- Real-time search
- Works in both galleries

### Photo Viewer
- Click any thumbnail
- Full-screen view
- All actions available
- Close with X or click outside
