# 🎉 Photo Booth - FIXED!

## ✅ What Was Fixed

The "Take Photo" button is now **ALWAYS ENABLED** when the camera is active. You no longer need face detection to take photos!

## 🚀 How to Use

### **Simple 3-Step Process:**

1. **Click "Start Camera"** 
   - Allow camera permissions when prompted
   - Wait for camera to start

2. **See your camera feed**
   - Blue box appears saying "Camera is Active"
   - You can now take photos immediately!

3. **Click "Take Photo"**
   - Works with or without face detection
   - Photo downloads automatically
   - Take as many photos as you want!

## 📸 What Happens Now

### **With Face Detection:**
- Green boxes appear around detected faces
- Button says: "Take Photo"
- Photo includes face bounding boxes
- Shows confidence scores

### **Without Face Detection:**
- Button says: "Take Photo (No Face Detected)"
- Photo still captures successfully
- No bounding boxes (just the photo)
- Works perfectly fine!

| Before | After |

## 💡 Pro Tips

### **For Best Results:**
- **Good lighting** helps face detection
- **Center your face** for better detection
- **But don't worry** - you can take photos even without detection!

### **Camera Not Starting?**
1. Allow camera permissions
2. Use HTTPS or localhost
3. Refresh the page
4. Try a different browser
